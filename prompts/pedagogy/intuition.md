# Intuition pass

A focused sub-prompt for `one_paragraph_intuition`. The intuition paragraph
must:

- Describe the system as if you were explaining it to a smart 14-year-old who
  has never seen the diagram.
- Use one strong metaphor at most. The metaphor must respect the mechanism
  (not just the shape).
- End with a sentence that primes the learner for the mechanism steps.
