# Study summary

Produce a tight markdown summary the learner can re-read in 90 seconds.

Sections, in order:

1. **One paragraph** — the gist.
2. **Mechanism in steps** — numbered list, 5–9 items.
3. **Why it matters** — 2–3 sentences.
4. **Watch out for** — common confusions and the right answer.
5. **Key takeaways** — 3–5 bullets.

Tone: precise, direct, no filler. Word count target: 250–400 words.
